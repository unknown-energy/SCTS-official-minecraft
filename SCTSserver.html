The server is a 24/7 online server with a maximum capacity of 50 members. You can peacefully build/play Minecraft with your friends. The server is only for the students studying in Sri Chaitanya Techno School. In the server, there is no rule of lifesteal, meaning there is no fear of getting banned from the server or the enemy gaining hearts via alt acc. However, you may get banned if any of the rules are broken and after 3 warnings. Scroll down for the IP.
